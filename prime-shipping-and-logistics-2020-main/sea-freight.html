Sea Freight

Reliable, cost-effective, and secure international shipping solutions for vehicles, electronics, and bulk goods — connecting you to every corner of the globe.

Global Shipping Made Simple

At Prime Shipping & Logistics, our Sea Freight service is designed for clients who need to move large volumes of goods internationally in the most efficient and economical way. Whether you’re shipping a single car, a container of electronics, or bulk commercial cargo, we provide tailored solutions to ensure safe and timely delivery across the globe.

We partner with leading ocean carriers and operate through major international ports, giving our clients flexible shipping options and competitive pricing. From secure loading at the port of origin to final delivery at the destination, we manage every stage of the process with professionalism and care.

Our Sea Freight Options

  • Full Container Load (FCL): Exclusive use of a container for large shipments.
  • Less than Container Load (LCL): Cost-sharing option where your goods travel in a consolidated container.
  • Roll-on/Roll-off (RoRo): Ideal for vehicles, trucks, and heavy machinery.
  • Break Bulk Shipping: For oversized or heavy cargo that doesn’t fit in containers.
